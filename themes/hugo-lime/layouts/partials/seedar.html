What We Do

SeedAR is one of the very first AR-based marketing solution providers in Iran. We are able to create marker-based, marker-less, and location-based augmented realities.

Marker-based

Pointing a camera at a specific trigger point (AR marker), the user can see and interact with life-like digital overlays and multimedia content.

Markerless

3D content overlaid right onto the user's surroundings. The most sophisticated form of augmented reality, markerless AR is based on the simultaneous localization and mapping (SLAM) technology.

Location-based

With aid of GPS, entering a particular location will trigger augmented 2D/3D animation, sound or video.
